About Me

I was born and raised in West Philadelphia. The area that I lived in was what some would call the wrong side of the tracks. My mom wanted the best for me and saw that I was going down a dangerous path. The last straw for her was when I got into an altercation with a few guys around my block. She immediately packed my things and sent me to live with my Aunt and Uncle in Bel Air. My new life gave me a chance to be a better person.
